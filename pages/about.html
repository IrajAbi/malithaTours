About Us

Explore the story and mission of Malitha Sri Lanka Tours

Welcome to Malitha Sri Lanka Tours

Founded in 2010, Malitha Sri Lanka Tours is a premier travel company specializing in authentic Sri Lankan experiences. Our passion for showcasing the beauty and culture of our island paradise drives everything we do.

With over a decade of experience, our team of expert local guides takes pride in crafting personalized journeys that reveal both the iconic landmarks and hidden gems of Sri Lanka. From the ancient ruins of Anuradhapura to the pristine beaches of Mirissa, we ensure every traveler experiences the true essence of our country.

What sets us apart is our commitment to sustainable tourism practices and deep connections with local communities. We believe in travel that not only creates unforgettable memories for our guests but also positively impacts the places we visit.

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, wildlife encounters, or cultural immersion, Malitha Sri Lanka Tours promises an authentic experience tailored to your preferences, all delivered with our signature Sri Lankan hospitality.

Our Story & Mission

Malitha Sri Lanka Tours was born from a deep love for our homeland and a desire to share its wonders with travelers from around the world. What began as a small family operation has grown into a trusted name in Sri Lankan tourism, while still maintaining our personal touch and attention to detail.

Our mission is simple: to provide authentic, responsible travel experiences that showcase the best of Sri Lanka while preserving its natural beauty and cultural heritage for generations to come.
